Protect And Care

NAVAJYOTI – A free creche for slum children aged between 3 year to 6 years. Main vision of this project is to give children, a secured and safe shelter from physical or verbal maltreatment, unjust practices or any type of aggression of society / neighbor. Provide protection in the absence of parents or guardians. As some of the cases Mother left the children, somewhere Man oust his wife and got married with another women and left the children unwanted and unattended . Some children lost both parents and live with their grandparents. Now livelihood of the parents of these children is Rag picker, domestic helper, daily labour, rickshaw puller etc.

Another mission is to get prepare the children’s for schools. Along with Design their behavior, give training on cleanliness. At present there are 25 children beneficiaries.
