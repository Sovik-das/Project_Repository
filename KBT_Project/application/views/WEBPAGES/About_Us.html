ABOUT US

Kripa Benevolent Trust is social welfare organization based in Kolkata, West Bengal started its philantrophic work for the underprivileged since 2017 and Registered under Trust Act in March 2018 We started our work with a prime objective to serve humanity in whichever manner it is conducive. Our belief is that every child should get basic education and everyone in the society should have access to proper healthcare and every senior citizen . Our vision is a society where Elderly people are respected as important member of the Society and not as a burden family member. They should be loved and cared , and our effort is to provide these fundamental rights to the best of our abilities and our means. Our Mission is to empower the most vulnerable ones of our society.

Aims and objectives of KRIPA BENEVOLENT TRUST

  • To establish OLD AGE HOME and strength up to maximum strength, so that we can help more senior citizens.
  • To promote welfare programmers for women, children, orphans, aged, blind and differently abled persons.
  • To provide medical facilities & health care to the needy.
  • To provide nutritious food to the inmates of the center.
  • To provide them periodically outing as picnic.
  • To provide shelter & clothing for the residents of the old age home.
  • To provide education to the needy children.
